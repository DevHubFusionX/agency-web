NEMVOL — Organizational Structure (Organogram)
Prepared by- Digitalife Ehub
This organogram reflects a lean, scalable structure for NEMVOL as a product
development agency. Roles and reporting lines are designed to maintain agility,
accountability, and professional delivery.

1. Leadership & Strategy – (CEO)
CEO / Founders - Provides overall vision, strategy, and growth direction -
Oversees client acquisition, partnerships, and investor relations - Approves
pricing, contracts, and high-level hiring decisions

2. Product Management & Operations Team – (PM)
Head of Product / Product Manager - Leads product discovery, validation, and
planning - Coordinates between clients, designers, and developers - Manages
product roadmap and iteration cycles
Manages internal processes, SOP adherence, and project delivery oversight -
Ensures operational efficiency and quality standards

Product Analysts / Associate PMs - Conduct market research, competitor
analysis, and user research - Support product definition and documentation

3. Design & User Experience Team – (PRODUCT DESIGNER)
Lead Designer / UX Lead - Oversees all design outputs, UX research, and visual
direction - Ensures brand consistency and user-centered design
UI/UX Designers - Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs -
Conduct usability testing and interface optimization
Design Intern / Assistant (optional) - Supports asset creation, updates, and
design documentation
Building People, Brands and purpose

4. Development & Engineering Team (CTO)
Lead Developer / Technical Lead - Oversees all development projects,
architecture, and code standards - Assigns tasks and ensures quality through
code reviews
Frontend Developers - Develop web and mobile interfaces - Integrate UI designs
and ensure responsiveness
Backend Developers - Build APIs, databases, and server-side logic - Ensure
performance, scalability, and security
Mobile Developers - Develop native or cross-platform mobile applications
QA Engineer / Tester - Conducts manual and automated testing - Ensures
delivery quality, bug-free releases

5. Sales, Marketing & Partnerships Team – (DIGITALIFE EHUB)
Business Development Lead / Sales Manager - Manages lead generation,
proposal creation, and client acquisition - Maintains relationships with agencies,
accelerators, and strategic partners
Marketing & Content Specialist - Develops website content, case studies, social
media, and thought leadership - Supports campaigns, PR, and brand visibility

6. Client Success & Support Team
Client Success Manager - Ensures smooth onboarding and project
communication - Monitors client satisfaction and retention
Support / Maintenance Engineers - Handles post-launch maintenance, updates,
and technical support tickets

Reporting & Communication Lines
 CEO → Product Lead, Design Lead, Sales Lead, Finance Lead
 Product Lead → Designers, Developers, QA, Analysts
 Sales Lead → Marketing Specialist, Partnerships

 Client Success → Project teams for delivery coordination
 All teams report KPIs, milestones, and status weekly to CEO/PM

Notes on Lean Scalability
 Some roles are multi-hatted at the early stage (e.g., CEO may handle
sales and product initially)
 Contractor or freelance developers/designers can fill gaps until full-time
hires are justified
 SOPs ensure accountability even in a lean setup

This organogram is designed to evolve as NEMVOL grows, maintaining clarity,
efficiency, and professional delivery standards.
